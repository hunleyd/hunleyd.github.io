Linka

October 3, 2016
  • kickstarter
  • biking

My Linka smart bike lock from Kickstarter showed up today. Holy Hell is this thing solid and hefty! Like for reals, this is gonna throw the balance of the bike off :)

I know what you're thinking. A smart bike lock? Why? Yeah, I know. However, even without the smart gimmicks, this is a nice self-contained bike lock. It mounts directly to the frame, is hella hefty and thick (1.6 pounds). Has a built in 'someone is fucking with me' alarm (that is quite ear piercing). It's made of through-hardened steel and has a square cross-section. And it doesn't stick out like a sore thumb when mounted.

Anyway, I'll edit this post later with thoughts after use.
