About

Born at the ass end of 1973, Douglas lived a (mostly) quiet life in and around the village of Weston, OH until moving to Columbus, OH to attend college in 1993. He acquired a taste for ‘city life’ during this time and has remained in the Greater Columbus Metro Area ever since.

Surviving both Guillain–Barré syndrome (GBS) and two teenage daughters are arguably his greatest achievements.

When not blogging, Douglas can be found curled up on the couch with the family Chihuahuas either reading a book on his tablet or watching a movie.
